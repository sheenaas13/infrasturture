Ecosystem Partnerships: Growing Together

Our strength lies not just within, but in the powerful bonds we build outside our walls. Through trusted collaborations and shared values.

Empowering Innovation Through Ecosystem Partnerships

In an increasingly interconnected world, ecosystem partnerships are essential for driving innovation and accelerating growth. By collaborating with a diverse network of organizations—including startups, enterprises, research institutions, and technology providers—we’re able to deliver more comprehensive, future-ready solutions.

These partnerships go beyond transactional relationships. They are built on shared goals, mutual trust, and a commitment to long-term success. Through open collaboration and co-creation, we amplify our impact and unlock new opportunities that benefit everyone in the ecosystem.

Trusted by Leading Industry Partners

We’re proud to collaborate with a diverse range of companies who share our vision for innovation, sustainability, and growth. These partnerships are more than symbolic—they represent real-world cooperation, shared success, and the collective drive to shape a better future.

Collaborative Project Highlights

01. Smart City Development with Bechtel

Partnered with Bechtel to develop cutting-edge smart infrastructure across urban centers. This initiative boosted energy efficiency, public safety, and digital connectivity.


Partnered with Bechtel, we embarked on a mission to reshape urban living through smart city development. The collaboration focused on integrating digital infrastructure, IoT solutions, and green technologies to create more efficient, responsive, and sustainable cities. Our joint effort brought smart lighting, waste management, and real-time traffic systems to life, transforming how residents interact with their environment.

02. Highway Expansion with L&T

In collaboration with Larsen & Toubro, we expanded 500+ kilometers of highway, improving trade routes and reducing traffic congestion across states.


In collaboration with Larsen & Toubro, we led a massive highway expansion initiative aimed at improving regional and national connectivity. The project added over 500 kilometers of new roadways, enhanced safety features, and introduced modernized tolling systems. This joint venture significantly boosted trade logistics and cut travel times for millions of commuters.

03. Green Energy Plant with Kiewit

Together with Kiewit, we launched a solar and wind hybrid energy facility, pushing boundaries in renewable resource management and clean energy innovation.


Together with Kiewit, we launched a groundbreaking hybrid renewable energy plant that harnesses both solar and wind power. Designed with efficiency and scalability in mind, the facility represents a major leap in clean energy generation. This partnership not only strengthened our commitment to sustainability but also helped local communities transition toward more reliable and eco-conscious energy sources.

04. Urban Metro Integration with Turner

Partnering with Turner Construction, we integrated modern metro lines across three cities, enabling faster, cleaner, and safer public transport solutions.


Our partnership with Turner Construction brought new life to metropolitan transit systems by expanding and integrating metro lines across three key cities. The project introduced smart ticketing, energy-efficient trains, and user-friendly connectivity between lines. As a result, public transportation became faster, safer, and more accessible—improving the daily commute for thousands.
